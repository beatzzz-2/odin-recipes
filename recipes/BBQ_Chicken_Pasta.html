BBQ Chicken Pasta

Description

This super speedy chicken pasta salad with broccoli, asparagus and peas is the perfect solution for busy weeknights.

Ingredients

  • 250g farfalle pasta
  • 200g snow peas, trimmed, halved lengthways
  • 1 bunch asparagus, woody ends trimmed
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 bunch baby broccoli
  • 1 roast chicken
  • 1/2 cup of peri-peri marinade
  • 1/4 cup mint springs
  • Lemon zest

Directions

  1. Cook the pasta in a large saucepan of boiling water following packet directions or until al dente, adding the snow pea, asparagus, peas and baby broccoli in the last 2 mins of cooking. Refresh under cold water. Drain well. ransfer to a large bowl.
  2. Add chicken to the pasta mixture. Drizzle with marinade. Toss to combine.
  3. Divide the pasta salad evenly among serving bowls. Sprinkle with mint sprigs. Season.
  4. Serve with lemon zest
